# **App Name**: The Savory Spoon

## Core Features:

- Header Branding: Display restaurant name and logo prominently.
- Navigation Bar: A navigation bar for the pages like Home, Menu, About, Contact Us, etc.
- Featured Dishes Section: Showcase daily specials or featured items with attractive images and descriptions.
- About Us Section: Provide details on the restaurant’s story, chefs, and ambiance.
- Contact Information: Include address, phone number, email, and a contact form for inquiries and reservations.
- Digital Menu: Display the full restaurant menu, divided into categories with descriptions and pricing.
- AI Wine Pairing Suggestions: An AI tool for suggesting wine pairings based on a customer's selected food items on the menu. Use tool-based reasoning to offer pairings that complement both the dish and the customer's preferences, enhancing their dining experience.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Rich burgundy (#800020) to evoke warmth and sophistication.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), a desaturated tone of the primary, for an inviting, soft backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted olive green (#808000) to complement the burgundy and add an earthy touch.
- Body font: 'Literata', a serif for readability and an intellectual feel.
- Headline font: 'Literata', a serif that harmonizes well with its use as body text.
- Elegant, simple icons for navigation and menu categories, in the olive green accent color.
- Clean, spacious layout with high-quality food photography to showcase dishes attractively.
- Subtle transitions and hover effects for an engaging user experience.
